Our Story

A Family Business Built on Doing the Job Right

Rideout Plumbing has been serving Charlotte and the surrounding area since 1981 — over 45 years of showing up, doing quality work, and standing behind it. Jason Rideout founded the company on one principle: be the plumber you'd actually want in your home.

Today Jason works alongside his son Chason and their crew, keeping the same family values that have earned them a 4.8-star rating and hundreds of repeat customers. No upselling, no surprises — just flat-rate honest pricing and a 1-year warranty on every job.
